History of the Internet in the Philippines

The internet was introduced to the Philippines in the early 1990s. The first connection was established on March 29, 1994, marking the country's official entry into the global network. This was made possible through the efforts of institutions like the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) and academic organizations.

Growth and Expansion

In the 2000s, the internet began to proliferate with the rise of internet caf√©s, affordable broadband packages, and the adoption of mobile internet. Social media platforms like Friendster, and later Facebook, became immensely popular, driving higher demand for connectivity.

Current Internet Use

As of today, the Philippines has one of the highest internet penetration rates in Southeast Asia. With millions of active social media users, the internet is a central part of daily life. However, challenges such as slow connection speeds and unequal access remain pressing issues.
